# Arcade Flow Analysis Report

## Summary

The user embarked on a journey to add the Razor A5 Lux scooter to their cart on Target.com, starting with a search and exploration of the product details. They considered different color options before making their choice and opting out of additional coverage. Finally, they reviewed their cart and even took a moment to watch a helpful video on the process.

## User Interactions

1. Clicked 'Get Started' button on the introductory chapter.
2. Clicked on the search bar on the Target homepage.
3. Typed a search query in the search bar.
4. Scrolled down the search results page for scooters.
5. Clicked on the Razor A5 Lux 2 Wheel Kick Scooter image to view details.
6. Scrolled down the product details page for the Razor A5 Lux scooter.
7. Clicked on the Blue color option for the Razor A5 Lux scooter.
8. Clicked on the Pink color option for the Razor A5 Lux scooter.
9. Clicked 'Add to cart' button for the Razor A5 Lux scooter.
10. Clicked 'Decline coverage' option for the protection plan.
11. Clicked on the cart icon to view selected items.
12. Watched a video about adding a scooter to the cart on Target.com for 27 seconds at 1.5x speed.
13. Clicked on an image on the cart page of Target.com.
14. Scrolled down the cart page on Target.com.
15. Clicked on 'Get started!' button to navigate to the Target homepage.
