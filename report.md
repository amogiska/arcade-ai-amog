# Arcade Flow Analysis Report

## Summary

The user embarked on a journey to find and add a Razor A5 Lux 2 Wheel Kick Scooter to their shopping cart on Target.com. Starting with a search on the homepage, they explored different color options for the scooter before deciding to add it to their cart. After declining additional coverage, they reviewed their cart, watched an informational video, and interacted with various elements on the page, ensuring everything was in order before proceeding to checkout.
