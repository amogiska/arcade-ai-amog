Content

Art Direction
- Flat, cheerful geometry; generous whitespace; crisp edges
- Palette: #0061FF (primary), #7FB3FF (accent), #111827 (text), #F8FAFC (bg)
- Subtle 8–12px soft shadow only for the main block; no heavy gradients
- Simple vector icon or abstract cut-outs; no photos or device mockups

Layout
- Asymmetric grid with one dominant color block
- Clear margins: 64px outer, 24px inner paddings
- Safe area: 5% on all sides
- Focal hierarchy: Headline > Subline > Badge (top-right or bottom-right)

Typography
- Inter or system sans; headline semibold, subline medium
- Headline size ~72–96px (1.3× subline), tight tracking
- High contrast text on solid fields; max 3–5 lines total

Production
- Export 1080×1080 and 1920×1080 PNG
- Provide both solid background (#F8FAFC) and transparent versions
- Stroke alignment to pixel grid; no raster effects beyond the soft shadow

Do / Don’t
- DO: Flat color fields, playful but minimal shapes, precise alignment
- DON’T: Photorealism, skeuomorphism, busy gradients, clip-art
